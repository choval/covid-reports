The updates(Feb. 11th) on novel Coronavirus in Korea
○ As of 09:00, 11 February 2020, there have been 3,629 suspected cases reported, 28
of which were confirmed, 2,736 turned out to be negative, and 865 currently being
tested. Out of 1,769 contacts of cases in total, 11 cases has been confirmed.
(since 3 January, 2020)
○ KCDC released the results of epidemiological investigation on 28th
confirmed case.
○ The 28th
case, a 30 year old Chinese female is close contact of 3th case and being
self-isolated on 26 January. The case had no onset of symptom(fever) during
self-isolated, since she has been taken a painkiller before being isolated.
- Since the result of test was indicated at the borderline between positive and
negative at the Incubation point, the re-test has been conducted. The case tested
positive on 10 February and isolated at national designated hospital. And the
resident of the contact tested negative.
○ Meanwhile, KCDC announces to expand strict quarantine screening to Hong Kong,
Macao on 12 February, in addition to those who arrive from mainland China
- As increased the number of confirmed case in Hong Kong, the spreading of the
disease in the community has been identified. And there is a high possibility on the
risk of transmission through Macao, nearby Guangdong province.
