The second imported case of 2019 novel coronavirus(2019-nCoV)
in Korea
○ The Korea Centers for Disease Control and Prevention(KCDC) has confirmed the second
imported case of 2019 novel coronavirus(2019-nCoV) from Wuhan, Hubei Province, China
on 24 January 2020.

○ The case is a 55 year old Korean male, who has been working in Wuhan. While case-patient
was working at Wuhan, he had initially developed cough and sore throat on 10 January 2020
then he visited the local hospital in China. Upon his arrival at Gimpo Airport, Korea via Shanghai
on 22 January 2020, the case was detected during entry screening conducted by the quarantine
station.

○ He was designated by active monitoring with fever, cough and sore throat. During this
active monitoring period, he visited a designated healthcare facility by the Public Health Center
on 23 January 2020 and confirmed positive for novel Coronavirus (2019-nCoV) on 24 January
2020 by sequencing at the Korea Centers for Disease Control and Prevention(KCDC).

○ KCDC said that “since the case-patient was fully aware Wuhan situation, he filled up well
the health questionnaire and cooperate with the public health during active monitoring. The
in-depth epidemiological investigation and contact tracing are underway. We will inform the
further detailed information as soon as it is available”.

○ Meanwhile, the World Health Organization(WHO) has decided not to consider the coronavirus
outbreak in China as a Public Health Emergency of International Concern(PHEIC) and its
emergency committee will reconvene in approximately 10 days.

○ As of Jan 24, 2020, 2 cases have been confirmed, and all 25 PUIs(Patient Under Investigation)
tested negative for the 2019-nCoV.
