One more imported case of 2019-nCoV has been confirmed in Korea
○ The Korea Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (KCDC) has confirmed 12th
case
of 2019 novel Coronavirus(2019-nCoV) on 31 January, 2020.
○ The confirmed case is a 49-year old Chinese male, who had visited Japan and returned
on 19 January 2020. Japanese confirmed case suggested to be examined. He tested positive
and was isolated in a national designated isolation hospital on 1 February 2020.
○ Contact tracing and in-depth epidemiological investigation is underway, and the results
will be shared with the public soon.
